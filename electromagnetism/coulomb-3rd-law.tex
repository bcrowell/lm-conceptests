\begin{qu}[The planetary model of the atom]\num
In the planetary model of the hydrogen atom, the electron is like a little planet in a circular orbit around the proton.
The electron's force on the proton\ldots

A. is zero.

B. is nonzero but much less than the proton's force on it.

C. has a strength equal to the proton's force on it.


\credit{Jack Dostal}
\end{qu}
